The Twelve Branches

Twelve interlocking sectors,
one living ecosystem.

RiverTree works across twelve interlocking sectors that restore the foundations of thriving human and ecological systems.

01
Health & Wellness
Whole-person health, preventative care, nervous system regulation, longevity, and integrative healing services.
02
Food & Nutrition
Local regenerative food systems, community kitchens, nutrient-dense food access, and food education.
03
Soil & Land Regeneration
Regenerative agriculture, soil restoration, biodiversity recovery, and water stewardship.
04
Regenerative Economy
Local enterprises, cooperative ownership models, ethical markets, and regenerative finance.
05
Education & Life Skills
Holistic education, youth development, apprenticeships, and practical life skills for all ages.
06
Housing & Living Environments
Healthy homes, regenerative eco-village development, tiny homes, and community-centered living.
07
Community & Culture
Arts, gatherings, shared rituals, social connection, and strengthening the social fabric.
08
Family & Intergenerational Support
Parenting support, elder care, youth mentorship, and multigenerational resilience.
09
Technology for Good
Ethical AI, regenerative digital platforms, coordination systems, and data for planetary wellbeing.
10
Environmental Stewardship
Climate resilience, water systems, land restoration, and ecological regeneration.
11
Local Governance & Civic Participation
Collaborative decision-making, community leadership development, and participatory governance.
12
Bioregional & Global Networks
Connecting regenerative hubs, regional supply chains, and planetary collaboration systems.

RiverTree integrates these twelve sectors into one living ecosystem — restoring the conditions for healthy people, thriving communities, and regenerative land systems.
